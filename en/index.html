Why this is different

Not just muscles.
I work with the whole system.

Hypopressive method is not just breathing. It's a precise rehabilitation technique that works with the coordination of the diaphragm, pelvic floor and nervous system as a whole.

  • Reduces intra-abdominal pressure — unlike classic exercises that increase it
  • Activates the deep stabilizing system without overloading the pelvic floor
  • Combines conscious movement, breath and nervous system work
  • Suitable postpartum, for diastasis, incontinence, prolapse and reflux
