About the course

What to expect

This course builds on a foundational knowledge of the hypopressive method. We focus on deepening breathing patterns, more precise body alignment and a gradual increase in the complexity of positions.

Over the 10 classes we work through the entire system — from body preparation through activation of the deep stabilising system to the conscious integration of breath, movement and the nervous system.

Every class is recorded. If you can't attend, you'll receive the recording and won't miss a thing. Missed classes can be made up in another scheduled session of the course.

Who is this course for?

For those who already have a basic knowledge of the hypopressive method — either from a beginner course or from individual sessions.

Where and when?

MotherGood studio, Sudoměřská 1243/25, Vinohrady. 5 time slots to choose from — Tuesday, Wednesday or Thursday.

How large is the group?

Small group — maximum 6–8 people. Each class is personal and Lucie gives you individual attention.

How a class looks

Structure of each class

01
~15–20 min · warm-up

Body preparation

The opening part of the class prepares the body for hypopressive technique work — we release tension and activate the necessary structures.

  • Strengthening and stretching
  • Balance exercises
  • Diaphragm self-massage
  • Conscious breathing and nervous system regulation
02
~35–40 min · technique

Hypopressive technique

The main part of the class — working with the hypopressive method in various positions, with an emphasis on precision and deepening of technique.

  • Breathing patterns and apnoea
  • Correct body alignment in positions
  • Creating the diaphragmatic vacuum
  • Progressively more demanding positions and sequences
Benefits

What the course brings

🧘

Better posture

Conscious work with the body's axis and the deep stabilising system carries over into everyday movement.

💪

Strong centre without overload

Activation of the body's core without increasing intra-abdominal pressure — a method that works with physiology, not against it.

🔥

Less back pain

Release of tension in the lumbar spine and neck thanks to the correct function of the diaphragm and pelvic floor.

💧

Functional pelvic floor

Strengthening and coordination of the pelvic floor without overloading — the key to managing incontinence and prolapse.

🌬️

Conscious breath

Deep connection with the breath as a tool for regulating both body and nervous system.

🌿

Overall vitality

Regular practice brings greater lightness, stability and energy into everyday life.
