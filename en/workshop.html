Your guides

Two practices,
two approaches, one intention.

Each workshop is led by a pair of specialists who complement each other. Together they connect body, breath and the nervous system.

Hypopressive method

Lucie Linková

Conscious work with intra-abdominal pressure, the diaphragm and the pelvic floor. Lucie guides you through a technique that activates the deep stabilising system — without force, without overload. The body learns to function more efficiently through breath and posture.

@bodyease_hypopressive
Pranayama · conscious breath

Katka

Pranayama is an ancient yogic practice of conscious breathwork — a direct gateway to the nervous system. Through extended exhale, breath retention and alternate nostril breathing, Katka calms the mind, regulates stress and deepens body awareness.

@breathart_katerina
